Home-Based Health Screening Packages

Can’t make it to a clinic? We’ll come to you! Our home-based checkups are perfect for busy individuals, families, or those who prefer the comfort of their own home.

Essential Screening

A quick and affordable checkup to monitor your vital signs, detect early risks, and give you a personalized wellness consultation.

Services Included:

  • Blood Pressure & Heart Rate
  • Blood Sugar Test: Random/Fasting
  • BMI & Weight Check
  • General Wellness Consultation

Teleconsultation:

  • Not included by default
  • Can be added if results require doctor review

Comprehensive Screening

A detailed health assessment that uncovers hidden risks, gives you deeper insights, and includes personalized nutrition and lifestyle guidance.

Services Included:

  • Everything in the Basic Package
  • Urine analysis (sugar, protein, infection indicators)
  • Nutrition & lifestyle counseling

Teleconsultation:

  • Includes a lite doctor teleconsultation (5–10 min)
  • Option to upgrade for a longer session

Family Wellness Package

(Up to 4 members & more)
A cost-effective way to check the whole family’s health in one visit, identify shared risks, and receive tailored household wellness advice.

Services Included:

  • Basic screening(Basic Package) for each family member
  • One summarized family health risk report
  • Free nutrition/lifestyle counseling session (household-focused)

Teleconsultation:

  • Includes a household teleconsultation (10–15 min) with a doctor
  • Option to add individual teleconsultations if needed
