Corporate Staff Screenings

Promote a healthy and productive workforce with our on-site corporate health screenings. Our dedicated team comes directly to your office, providing comprehensive and convenient checkups designed to ensure your staff stay healthy and engaged.

Essential Wellness

The perfect entry point for organizations looking to start their employee wellness journey.

  • Core Vitals: Blood pressure, temperature, BMI, and random blood sugar.
  • Hemoglobin Test: A quick screening to check for anemia.
  • Lifestyle & Nutrition Counseling: A brief 10-15 minute session for personalized advice.

Comprehensive Wellness

Addresses both physical and mental health, providing a more holistic employee wellness snapshot.

  • Everything in Essential Package, plus:
  • Vision & Hearing Screening: Basic tests to detect common sensory issues.
  • PHQ-9 Depression Screening: A widely used tool to screen for depression symptoms.
  • Stress Perception Scale: An assessment to help employees manage their stress levels.

Executive Wellness

Best suited for organizations keen on long-term employee performance and retention.

  • Everything in Comprehensive Package, plus:
  • Burnout Inventory: Assesses and helps prevent professional burnout.
  • Ergonomics & Workplace Checklist: Advice on improving comfort and reducing strain.
  • Personalized Health Recommendations: Tailored reports for each employee.
  • Company-wide Health Report: Anonymized summary of overall health trends.
