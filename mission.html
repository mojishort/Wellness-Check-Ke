Our Mission and Vision

Wellness Check Ke is a private mobile health screening company dedicated to bringing affordable, preventive healthcare closer to people where they live, learn, and work. We provide accessible health screenings in workplaces, schools, communities, and homes, focusing on early disease detection and holistic wellness.

What sets us apart is our integration of technology-driven services such as mobile apps, SMS results, and digital health tracking. We also ensure continuity of care through follow-up support with our partner hospitals—bridging the gap between screening and long-term wellness.

Mission Statement

"At Wellness 360 Kenya, our mission is to make preventive healthcare accessible, convenient, and technology-driven. Through mobile health screenings, digital health tracking, and seamless follow-up care, we empower individuals, organizations, and communities to take charge of their well-being. Our goal is to detect health risks early, promote holistic wellness, and create a healthier future for all."

Vision Statement

"To be Kenya’s most trusted and innovative mobile health service provider, leading the way in preventive care, early detection, and holistic wellness."

Bringing quality, affordable healthcare to your doorstep. Mobile screenings, corporate wellness, and tech-driven health solutions.
