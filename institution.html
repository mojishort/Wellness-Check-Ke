Faith-Based Institutions, Schools & Universities

We bring health screenings to your community hubs, ensuring students, staff, and congregants have access to essential health services.

Faith-Based Institutions

We partner with faith-based institutions (churches, mosques, temples, and other places of worship) to bring wellness to their communities

  • Blood pressure & blood sugar checks
  • BMI & weight checks
  • Basic eye & hearing screening
  • Mental health awareness talks (stress, depression, addictions)
  • Nutrition & wellness counseling
  • Maternal & child health talks
  • Referrals (for identified health issues)
  • Dental checks (Add-on)
  • Pharmacy / supplements stand (Add-on)

Schools (Primary & Secondary)

Promoting a healthy learning environment for students.

  • Growth monitoring (height, weight, BMI)
  • Vision screening
  • Hearing tests
  • Mental wellness & hygiene talks (stress, bullying, puberty, self-esteem)
  • Nutrition & hygiene education
  • Referrals (for further care)
  • Dental check-ups (Add-on)

Training Institutions (Universities & Colleges)

Supporting the health and well-being of young adults.

  • Lifestyle disease screening (BP, sugar, BMI)
  • Mental health screening (stress, depression, anxiety questionnaires)
  • Sexual & reproductive health awareness (Youth Friendly Services, STI prevention, family planning)
  • Fitness & nutrition counseling
  • Alcohol & substance abuse awareness
  • Referrals (for further care)
  • Counseling sessions (Add-on)
