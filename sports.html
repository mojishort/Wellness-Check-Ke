Sports & Fitness Health Checks

Assess, Optimize, Protect
At Wellness Check KE, our healthcare team brings professional fitness and health screenings directly to gyms, schools, workplaces, and sports teams. We identify cardiovascular, musculoskeletal, and performance risks, providing actionable insights to improve fitness, prevent injuries, and optimize overall health.

Essential Package

For fitness enthusiasts, corporate & school teams, gyms

  • Baseline Fitness & Health Screening: Resting Heart Rate, Blood Pressure, Oxygen Saturation, Respiratory Rate, BMI, and waist-to-hip ratio.
  • Musculoskeletal & Injury Risk Screening: Posture assessment, flexibility & range of motion tests, core stability, balance tests, and functional movement observation. Identification of red flags.
  • Cardiovascular & Endurance Check: Step Test, Talk Test, and observation of fatigue and breathing patterns.
  • Performance Optimization & Recovery Guidance: Exercise intensity recommendations, warm-up & cool-down routines, recovery strategies, and basic nutrition advice.
  • Mental Performance Screening: Identify stress, focus, or motivation risks with brief guidance and referral if needed.

Deliverables: Personalized Fitness & Injury Risk Report with exercise, recovery, and lifestyle recommendations.

Comprehensive Package

For high-performance athletes, competitive teams, and complete evaluations

  • Includes all Essential Package services PLUS:
  • Advanced Musculoskeletal Assessment: Manual muscle testing, joint-specific stress tests, and physiotherapy referrals if needed.
  • Advanced Cardiovascular Screening: VO₂ max estimation and ECG.
  • Mental Performance Screening: Identify stress, focus, or motivation risks with brief guidance and referral if needed.

Deliverables: Comprehensive Fitness & Injury Risk Report with detailed specialist recommendations, exercise plans, and recovery guidance.

Team Fitness Package

Wellness & Performance for Groups (10+ participants)

  • All Essential Package and Comprehensive screenings, delivered in a group format.
  • Efficient bulk health checks for 10+ participants.
  • Discounted group pricing for affordable wellness access.

    • Exclusive Benefits for Gyms & Teams:

    • Co-branded reports with your gym/team’s name.
    • A Group Wellness Summary Report with overall trends & risks.
    • “Wellness Day” events to boost member engagement & enrollment.

Deliverables: Individual fitness & injury risk reports, group insights & recommendations, and options for recurring screenings.
