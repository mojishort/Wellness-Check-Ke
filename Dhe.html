About the Program

At Wellness Check KE, we believe health education should be simple, accessible, and impactful. Through our Digital Health Educator program, we provide communities with trusted, practical health knowledge delivered straight to their phones, schools, and workplaces.

How We Deliver Health Education

  • SMS & WhatsApp Updates – quick, reliable health tips in your pocket
  • Social Media Campaigns – engaging posts, videos, and infographics
  • Virtual Webinars & Live Talks – interactive health sessions with experts
  • Community Challenges – fitness, mental health, and nutrition awareness campaigns

Focus Areas

  • Cancer Awareness & Screening
  • Maternal & Newborn Care
  • Mental Health & Wellness
  • Lifestyle Diseases (Diabetes, Hypertension)
  • Preventive Health & Nutrition

Why It Matters

  • Easy-to-understand health information
  • Reaches both urban & rural communities
  • Promotes prevention over cure
  • Builds healthier, more informed families and workplaces
