VIP Home Health Checkup

World-class healthcare, delivered to your doorstep. Exclusive care, unmatched privacy.

Overview

Our VIP Home Health Checkup is designed for clients who value convenience, privacy, and premium care. With a licensed doctor on-site, advanced diagnostics, and personalized wellness planning, this package ensures a world-class healthcare experience from the comfort of your home.

Services Included

Comprehensive health screening (CBC, sugar, cholesterol, kidney/liver function tests)

Vital signs monitoring (BP, pulse, temperature, oxygen saturation)

ECG (heart health assessment)

Urinalysis

On-site doctor consultation & immediate interpretation of results

Personalized health & wellness plan (diet, fitness, lifestyle guidance)

Detailed health report with recommendations

Unique Value & Benefits

  • Doctor-led Care: A premium, doctor-led home visit for a world-class healthcare experience.
  • Privacy & Comfort: Enjoy full privacy and peace of mind in the comfort of your own space.
  • Time-Saving: Avoid hospital queues and waiting rooms. Your time is valuable.
  • All-in-One Checkup: A comprehensive, one-stop health solution in a single appointment.
  • ASeamless Follow-up: Effortless follow-up care with our partnered hospitals when needed.

Target Audience

  • Busy professionals with demanding schedules
  • VIP clients who prefer exclusive and discreet healthcare
  • Elderly individuals seeking comfort and convenience
  • Families or executives needing personalized, preventive healthcare

Optional Add-Ons

Quarterly VIP check-ins and follow-up visits

Home monitoring devices (BP monitor, glucometer, wellness gadgets)

Executive wellness coaching (nutrition, fitness, stress management)

Advanced genetic / specialized lab tests

Teleconsultations with specialist doctors
