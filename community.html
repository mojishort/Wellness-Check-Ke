Community & Rural Health Outreach

Bringing Healthcare to the Heart of Communities
At Wellness Check Kenya, we believe that no one should be left behind when it comes to health. Our mobile medical team travels to underserved areas, delivering essential health screenings, education, and support where it’s needed most.

1. Partnerships with NGOs & County Governments

We collaborate with NGOs, development partners, and county health departments to deliver thematic medical outreaches that align with their mandates. Whether it’s maternal and child health, youth wellness and drug awareness, or elderly care, we provide the logistics, professional staff, and health solutions — while our partners provide funding and community mobilization.

Why partner with us?

  • Ready-to-deploy mobile health units
  • Professional healthcare teams
  • Data collection & reporting for accountability
  • Flexible outreach packages tailored to partner goals

Position your organization as a catalyst for healthier communities while we handle the execution.

2. Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Programs

We empower companies to transform their CSR budgets into life-changing community health programs. By sponsoring health camps in rural areas, your brand not only reaches thousands but also creates lasting social impact.

CSR Opportunities:

  • Sponsor Branded Rural Medical Camps
  • Partner on Thematic Health Awareness Days (Breast Cancer, Prostate Cancer, School Health Week, etc.)
  • Long-term community trust and loyalty

Are you an NGO, county government, or corporate looking to make an impact?
Partner with Wellness Check Ke to deliver community health where it’s needed most.
